The Potential Medal Winners and Competitors of Taiwan at Historical Olympic Games

Data Mining

Abstract

In history, human made a competition through sport games, well-known as Olympic Games. This sport competition has been a common interaction between people of different races and different countries. With the development of training equipment and strategy, the body shape and characteristic of players in Olympic Games have gradually improved in a proper way. We aim to find the potential medal winners of Taiwan and increase the probability for Taiwanese players to win a prize in Olympic. To reach the goal, we analyze the winner record in historical Olympic Games from 1896 to 2016. In section I, we conduct the initial data analysis through adding useful information into dataset, filtering data with the critical criterion, and visualizing data. Based on the initial data analysis, three kinds of topic are proposed: the most competitive sports for Taiwan, the prediction of potential medal winner, and the analysis of potential competitors from different country. The methodology and the analyzing result are discussed in section III and section IV respectively. The conclusion, given in section V, summarizes the three insights: 1. the men’s swimming, men’s shooting, women’s athletics, and women’s badminton are the most competitive sport game for Taiwanese players. Therefore, the training center for Olympic Games in Taiwan may highly emphasize those four types of games, which may win Taiwan a medal. 2. The three key factors to predict the potential medal winner are gender, country, and types of the games. 3. The Taiwanese weightlifting players must be cautious when competing with the Chinese players whose height is lower than 156.5 centimeter, and weight is lower than 56.5 kilogram. In addition, the Taiwanese taekwondo players must be cautious as well if they meet the Korean player or Thai player.

IPO model

01. Input

  • The winner record of historical Olympic Games

02. Process

  • Utilize association rule learning methodology to find the most competitive sports for Taiwan
  • Apply random forest algorithm, Naive Bayes classifier, logistic regression, SVM to predict the potential medal winner, and use accuracy and F1 score as the evaluation criterion
  • Use decision tree to find the potential competitors of Taiwanese player

03. Output

  • the information of the most competitive sports for Taiwan
  • the prediction result of potential medal winner
  • the information of the potential competitors of Taiwan from different country

Benefit and Result

  • Offer additional information to the training center for Olympic Games in Taiwan
