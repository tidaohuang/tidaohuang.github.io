Introduction

Tidao Huang is a fresh graduate of 2020 from National Tsing Hua University (NTHU) with the major of industrial engineering and engineering management (IEEM). His area of interests includes operation research (optimization) and data analytics, and he spent most of his junior and senior year on taking the relevant courses of the two areas to meet the requirement of job opportunity in the near future. His previous working experience includes service as the director of the IEEM summer camp from 2017 to 2018, where he planned and arranged a 6-day activity for high school students with more than 60 college students at IEEM department. He served as an intern at the department of information technology in AzureWave Technology, Inc., Taipei, Taiwan from September, 2018 to June, 2020 while completing his Bachelor degree in NTHU. During his internship, he experienced scheduling system maintenance with SQL and C#, regression analysis of the number of direct/indirect labors, optimization of feed-material processes, and visualization of electricity usage of a factory with Plotly and Dash. Now, he is ready for a job related to either operation research or data analytics.
